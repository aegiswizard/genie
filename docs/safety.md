# Genie 🧞‍♀️ — Safety & Ethics

## What Genie is

Genie is a best-effort state sketch tool. It observes acoustic and visual patterns
in short clips and returns probability estimates over communication state labels.

## What Genie is not

- Not a mind reader
- Not a lie detector
- Not a medical diagnostic tool
- Not a psychological assessment
- Not ground truth about any person's internal state

## The one sentence that covers everything

> "This is a best-effort state sketch from observable signals."

## Prohibited uses

Do NOT use Genie for:
- Medical or psychiatric diagnosis
- Legal proceedings
- Hiring or employment decisions
- Law enforcement or policing
- Surveillance without consent
- Any decision with significant consequences for an individual

## Disclaimer (always present in output)

Every GenieResult includes:

> "Best-effort state sketch from observable signals. Not mind reading.
> Not medical, legal, or psychological assessment."

## Consent

Always obtain informed consent before analyzing another person's voice or video.
Genie is designed for self-analysis, team tools with consent, and research contexts.

## Accuracy

Genie uses heuristic acoustic analysis and optional ML models.
False positives and false negatives are expected and normal.
Confidence levels reflect signal quality, not certainty of truth.
A "high confidence" result means the signals are clear — not that the label is correct.
